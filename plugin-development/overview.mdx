Amplication's **Plugin Development** framework empowers organizations to tailor their development platform while maintaining centralized control, creating a virtuous cycle between developer autonomy and architectural governance.

## Why Plugins Matter for Platform Teams

In enterprise environments, standardization and customization must coexist. Amplication's plugin system enables you to:

  <Card title="Enforce Golden Paths" icon="road">

  <Card title="Integrate Your Existing Systems" icon="link">
    Connect to internal auth providers, monitoring tools, and legacy systems
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your Technology Stack" icon="code">
    Amplication adapts to any technology stack without disrupting existing workflows
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share Expertise" icon="share">
    Package and share internal apps, integrations, and knowledge as private plugins
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
